doggo the helper

a multi-purpose, free and open source discord bot, built for your needs

here are some of its features:



starboards

show off the most unforgettable moments on your server, just use "/starboard add" to begin (supports up to 5 starboards!)



postboards

auto thread your messages and separate conversations from the real stuff easily.



anti-spam (honeypots)

set up honeypots in an instant, no dashboard needed



giveaways

create, manage or participate in giveaways on your server!



economy system (coinz)

have fun with the economy system with /dailycoinz, /slots, /coinflip, /pay and more!



fun commands

includes /8ball, /catfacts, /quote, and more to entertain you



...and useful ones too!

such as /qrcode, /base64, /remind, /hash, and /embed.



linked channels

sync chats between 2 servers at the same time with /link



counting game

count together with your members to see if you can reach the goal!
